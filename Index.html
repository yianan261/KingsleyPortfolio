Andriod Attendance App Design


Created an attendance Tracker Application with AndriodStudio, SQLite, Gradle and espresso. This was to provide professors a tool to track student attendance daily without having to take roll calls each class. With the use of NFC tags, which was hosted through AWS cloud service, students where able to sign into their respective classes daily and be marked present atomatically on the app. And becasue they had to phsically scan the NFC tags, the professor knows they where present
